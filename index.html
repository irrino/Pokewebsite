Pokemon Card

I, the undersigned collector, affirm that the Pokémon card collection described herein has been assembled through my own personal efforts over an extended period of time. The collection consists of authentic Pokémon Trading Card Game (TCG) cards originating from various officially licensed printing regions, including but not limited to English-language releases printed for distribution in the United States and other international markets, as well as selected cards from additional authorized regional printings when available. The cards within this collection represent a broad range of Pokémon sets, expansions, promotional releases, special collections, and rarity levels, including common, uncommon, rare, holo rare, ultra rare, full art, alternate art, secret rare, and other specialty cards recognized by the Pokémon TCG community.

I further state that all cards included in this collection were personally acquired by me and were not obtained through inheritance, bulk transfers, or third-party ownership arrangements. Approximately ninety-five percent (95%) of the cards in this collection were pulled directly by me from factory-sealed Pokémon booster packs, elite trainer boxes, collection boxes, tins, and other officially distributed sealed Pokémon products. These cards were obtained through legitimate retail purchases and opened by me personally, ensuring a direct chain of acquisition from sealed product to collection storage.
